## China's "George Washington(s)"
Well, we all remember *our guy*.  GW squared off with the British, kicked their ass with a rag-tag crew of hitters, everyone in the neighborhood said "You're The Dude", Jefferson mailed a letter to King George that said "Go Fuck Yourself" and off we went.

Not quite that simple with China but there are some parallels.  I like parallels, in case you haven't noticed.  China has had a few *uprisings* so there are multiple GWs.  But, a few stand out and I'll just focus on those:

**The guy who said "this is all China now" is....Qin Shi Huang**

Back in the day, prior to 221 BC, it was just a bunch of kingdoms and warlords.  They all hated each other.  One of them was this Qin dude.  He must of really hated the others because, at like 25 or something, he decided "Imma take over all the others"...and did.  Put "First Divine Emperor" on his business card and called the shop "China".

Bold, but pretty dang smart too.  Because, after he took everything over, he unified the lot. Standard currency, language, weights and measures, and even a legal system.  He actually standardized the axels of horse carts so they can all use his standardized roads. Like, this guy really knew his shit.

The problem with his set up was that it wasn't built for the long haul.  It was his Hoagie Hut and he didn't train his kids well enough to take it over.  So, they fucked it all up and it kinda shuttered for a while then went up for sale.

For, like, the next 2000 years, the Hut was had different ownerships (aka: "dynasties"). Some good, some bad, but you can read about them in the Fact Sheet. (CLAUDE, link here)

Then, on November 12th, 1866, a guy named Sun Yat-sen was born.  A fellow Scorpio, I might add.  I would have loved to party with this dude.  Like, mad props coming so enjoy reading about this guy.

Born in a tiny farming village in the sticks of China, it's kinda what you expect.  Dirt poor, ate what they grew, trying not to die.  At the time, Hawaii was pumping out sugar like [need cute analogy here].  They needed workers and actually recruited from places like China.  Sun Yet's older brother, Sun Mei, hoped on one of the boats and headed to paradise to pick sugar for the Hawaiian Kingdom (remember, this was before we annexed them).  But, the older brother must have been pretty smart as he took that contract labor job and, somehow, turned it into owning 12,000 acres of Hawaiian farmland himself.  Got filthy rich.  

The last dynasty, which Sun Yet and his brother lived in, was the Qing dynasty.  Everything was going to hell in that time and, if they had confidence and sentiment scores [link to my fact sheet], they would have been in the toilet.  So, the older brother jumped that ship, got on another one, and made something of himself.  Then, he brought over little brother to Hawaii and funded his education.

From ages 13-17, in Hawaii, Sun Yet learns english, math, science, Abraham Lincoln, American Governance, his brother's business, and.....Christianity.  He dug that religion so much, he wanted to get himself baptized.  See, back then, converting to Christianity was seen as turning your back on your Chinese ancestors, a huge no no.  Some Confucius thing.  So, his older brother went "nope" and shipped his ass back.

On the way back, Sun Yet couldn't stop thinking about how much he'd learned, from the West, and why China couldn't do some of that shit.  Not like full on cut-and-paste but the good stuff.   So he rolls back into his hometown and starts running his mouth.  "Why not this?  Why not that?  The Qing's suck at their job.  Why are we worshiping like this?"   Then, ransacks the temple, pisses off the locals some awful and they kick his ass out.

Sun Yet moves to Beijing, studies medicine and becomes a doctor, making fat stacks.  But, still is running is mouth and decides to try and offer his assistance to the Qing's for some "changes".  He writes a letter, drops it off at the Qing's headquarters and never hears back.  Not even some boiler plate "Thank you, we'll review your request and get back to you."  Nothing.

For some reason, that pissed him off something awful.  So much so that he decides....to start a revolution.  Yeah, kinda just like that.  "Fuck them Qing's!  Let's change this up."
